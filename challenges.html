The Current Situation

Many children with mobility impairments face significant barriers in daily life. The lack of child-friendly mobility aids impacts education, play, and psychological well-being.

Key Issues:

  • Limited availability of indigenous products in our country.
  • Designs often lack emotional appeal and user engagement.
  • Products fail to evolve with the growing needs of children.
  • Mobility aids are viewed as medical devices, not companions.
